The Veil’s Edge

“In the darkest alleys and beneath the flickering lantern light, death wears a shadow’s smile.” Cloaked in midnight and moving like a whisper, The Veil’s Edge is the master of silent death, feared even among the most seasoned of killers. Armed with the Dagger of Tenebris, a blade forged in the heart of an eclipse, she is the embodiment of darkness, her strikes so swift and precise that foes fall before they feel the sting. She drifts through the night, unseen and unheard, guided by the spectral whispers of spirits long departed. Her touch is poison, her steps a dance of shadows; each kill is a work of art, precise and untraceable. No lock can bar her way, no armor can stay her blade. She is a specter that passes through walls and whispers into dreams, the unseen terror that haunts the underbelly of kingdoms. To glimpse her is to court death; to be her target is to embrace oblivion.
