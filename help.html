Ultimate Showdown - Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I start a game?

To start a game, both players need to shuffle their decks and draw five cards. Decide who goes first by flipping a coin or rolling a die. Then, follow the rules outlined in the “How to Play” section.

What should I do if my opponent disconnects?

If your opponent disconnects during an online match, the game will automatically pause. If they fail to reconnect within 5 minutes, you may either end the game or wait until they rejoin.

How can I report a bug?

If you encounter a bug during gameplay, please use the contact form below to report it. Make sure to provide detailed information about the issue and, if possible, include screenshots.

Can I play the game offline?

Yes! Ultimate Showdown has an offline mode where you can play against AI opponents. However, some features like multiplayer and leaderboard updates require an active internet connection.

How do I unlock new cards?

New cards can be unlocked by winning matches, completing in-game achievements, or by purchasing expansion packs. Head over to the in-game store to view available packs.

Troubleshooting

  • Game won’t load: Make sure your internet connection is stable. Try refreshing the page or restarting the app.
  • Game freezes: Close the game and reopen it. Ensure your device meets the game’s minimum system requirements.
  • Audio not working: Check your device’s sound settings. Make sure the audio is not muted in-game or on your device.
  • Cannot log in: Double-check your username and password. If you’ve forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" option to reset it.
